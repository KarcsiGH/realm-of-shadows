# Realm of Shadows — Background Art
# SESSION A: Town Exteriors (11 images)

---

## BEFORE YOU START — READ THIS CAREFULLY

This session generates exactly 11 images. You will generate them **one at a time**, display each as a preview, and only move on after it is complete. Do not batch-generate. Do not skip previews.

### Style Reference

Open these two files from the game folder and look at them before you paint anything:
- `~/Documents/RealmOfShadows/assets/sprites/characters/Paladin.png`
- `~/Documents/RealmOfShadows/assets/sprites/characters/Spellblade.png`

**Paint the backgrounds in exactly the same artistic style as these character sprites.**

Analysing these sprites: they are painted digital illustrations with flat colour areas separated by hard dark outlines. Each material (metal, skin, fabric, stone) has 3-5 tones of that colour used as flat fills. Hard edges between major regions — no soft airbrush blending between colours. Black or near-black outlines on major forms. Rich detail within each zone. Graphic, confident, dark fantasy. Like a high-quality comic book panel or Darkest Dungeon concept art.

**This is NOT pixel art with large blocks. This is full-resolution painted illustration in a graphic style.**

### Canvas Size
All town exteriors: **1440 × 900 pixels**

### Quality Bar
These backgrounds will appear behind the game's UI. They need to feel like real places — atmospheric, dark fantasy, painted with care. Each should take real effort to paint, not be generated in seconds.

### Image 1: `briarhollow.png`

**Scene:** The starting village. Stone-and-timber buildings around a muddy square. Evening light, warm amber lanterns glowing in windows. An inn visible (two storeys, timber frame, hanging lantern sign). A smithy with a small chimney. Dense dark forest pressing in from three sides, fog drifting at the treeline. One or two stars in a dark blue-grey sky. The foreground is muddy earth, a dirt road leading into the scene.

**Mood:** Humble, slightly melancholy. A quiet village at the edge of the world.

**Key elements:**
- 3-5 stone-and-timber buildings, warm lantern glow in windows
- Dark forest wall behind the buildings
- Fog threading between the trees
- Muddy foreground, dirt road
- Deep blue-grey night sky

**Palette:** Night sky `#2A3448`, forest dark green `#1A2C10`, stone grey `#8A8070`, timber brown `#6B4020`, lantern amber `#D08020`, mud `#7A6040`, fog `#C8C8C0`

### Image 2: `woodhaven.png`

**Scene:** A village built into massive ancient trees. Buildings are platforms in the branches connected by rope bridges. The base of the great trunks is at ground level with more structures. A communal fire pit glows orange in a forest clearing. Glowing mushrooms add blue-green light. The canopy is so thick it almost blocks the sky — this feels like being inside a living cathedral of wood.

**Mood:** Mysterious, organic, beautiful but unsettling.

**Key elements:**
- Massive tree trunks as the dominant vertical forms
- Rope bridges between elevated platforms
- Fire pit glow from below
- Glowing mushrooms (bioluminescent blue-green)
- Dense canopy overhead, almost no sky visible

**Palette:** Forest black-green `#0E1C08`, bark brown `#5A3818`, fire orange `#E06020`, mushroom glow `#80C030`, amber lantern `#C08020`, deep shadow `#060C04`

### Image 3: `ironhearth.png`

**Scene:** A dwarven mining town squeezed into a narrow mountain valley. Low, heavily-built stone structures with iron-banded shutters and reinforced doors. The Deep Forge dominates — a massive industrial building with a tall chimney belching orange-lit smoke. Mining cart tracks run through the street. Iron bracket lanterns on every building. Everything coated in soot. Mountains press close on both sides — the sky is a narrow strip above.

**Mood:** Industrial, oppressive, proud. These people chose to live inside a mountain.

**Key elements:**
- Low stone buildings with iron fittings
- Dominant forge chimney belching glowing smoke
- Mining cart tracks in the street foreground
- Mountain walls flanking both sides
- Narrow sky strip, everything below in shadow

**Palette:** Soot stone `#4A4040`, iron `#707070`, forge smoke glow `#D06020`, coal `#1E1818`, lamp amber `#C08020`, mountain dark `#302828`, sky strip `#1A2030`

### Image 4: `crystalspire.png`

**Scene:** A city of slender arcane towers at night. Crystal observatories at the tower tops glow with cold blue-white light. The cobblestone streets have geometric ley line patterns that glow faintly blue. Robed scholar silhouettes visible in tower windows. Stars unnaturally numerous and bright in the sky. The whole city has a blue-silver luminescence that feels both beautiful and wrong.

**Mood:** Intellectually beautiful, slightly unsettling. The city knows things it shouldn't.

**Key elements:**
- Multiple slender towers with glowing crystal tops
- Blue ley line patterns in the cobblestones
- Robed silhouettes in windows
- Overcrowded night sky with too many stars
- Blue-silver ambient glow throughout

**Palette:** Midnight `#0E0E28`, crystal glow `#5070C0`, tower stone `#404858`, cobble `#505060`, arcane blue `#7090FF`, silver `#C0C8D0`, star white `#E8F0FF`

### Image 5: `thornhaven.png`

**Key elements:**
- Wide boulevard receding to a gatehouse in the background
- Red banners with gold sun symbol hanging from lamp posts
- Noble townhouses on both sides
- Guard towers and gatehouse in midground
- Cracked fountain in foreground

**Palette:** Imperial red `#802020`, gold sun `#C09020`, cobble `#787060`, stone `#908880`, overcast sky `#707888`, lamp amber `#C08020`, dark window `#1A1820`

### Image 6: `sanctum.png`

**Scene:** The holy city at twilight. Viewed from the processional avenue, looking toward the Grand Cathedral facade at the far end. The Cathedral is white stone with soaring height — its tall stained window arches glow from within in red, gold, and blue. Thousands of candle-stands line both sides of the avenue. Pilgrim silhouettes moving in both directions. The sky directly behind and above the Cathedral glows unnaturally bright even as darkness falls elsewhere.

**Mood:** Awe, overwhelming scale, devotion. The architecture makes you feel small deliberately.

**Key elements:**
- Long avenue converging on the Cathedral (strong perspective)
- Cathedral facade at the vanishing point — massive, white stone, stained windows glowing
- Candle-stands creating twin rivers of warm light along the avenue
- Pilgrim silhouettes in both directions
- Dramatically bright sky behind the Cathedral

**Palette:** Cathedral white-cream `#E8E0D0`, stained glass red `#901820` / gold `#C09020` / blue `#2040A0`, candle amber `#D08820`, pilgrim dark `#2A2020`, avenue stone `#B0A898`, divine sky `#F0E8C8`

### Image 7: `saltmere.png`

**Scene:** A rough port town on a grey sea, overcast and drizzling. Weathered wooden docks extending into dark choppy water. A fishing boat and a battered merchant vessel tied up. **Key elements:**
- Docks extending into dark water (foreground)
- Boats at dock — weathered, beaten
- Barrels, rope, nets on the docks
- Low waterfront buildings, one with dim interior light
- Grey overcast sky, rain visible as diagonal lines

**Palette:** Sea grey-green `#607080`, wet dark wood `#4A3828`, rope tan `#9A8050`, dark water `#283848`, dim lamp `#C07820`, rain sky `#707880`, salt foam `#90A8B0`

### Image 8: `greenwood.png`

**Scene:** A wilderness outpost at dusk. A clearing in deep forest with three or four sturdy wooden buildings — a ranger post, a supply cache with a reinforced door, a small shrine post. A large campfire in the clearing centre. The forest walls are dark and close on all sides. The sky through the clearing is orange-purple twilight — the only visible sky in the image. The forest feels like it's watching.

**Mood:** Isolated, exposed, last safe stop. The forest is dominant, not the settlement.

**Key elements:**
- Small cluster of functional buildings
- Campfire as the light source and visual centre
- Forest walls dominating all four sides — very close
- Orange-purple twilight sky visible only in the clearing gap above
- The clearing itself feels small, the forest enormous

**Palette:** Forest dark `#1A2C10`, firepit orange `#C06820`, timber `#6B4020`, twilight sky `#6040A0` fading to `#E08030`, earth `#4A3828`, shadow `#0A1008`

### Image 9: `emberveil.png`

**Scene:** A volcanic outpost under permanent orange-grey ash sky — there is no night here, lava provides constant light. Low basalt buildings built against a volcanic ridge. Lava channels run between the black rocks, glowing intense orange-red. Steam vents create drifting white columns. Renn's Forge is the largest structure, its massive chimney adding smoke to the already thick air. The entire sky is a haze of ash and volcanic glow — no stars, no moon, just perpetual orange-grey.

**Mood:** Hostile, visceral, dangerous. People live here because they have nowhere else or because they don't mind the heat.

**Key elements:**
- Lava channels between rocks — these are the primary light source
- Basalt buildings (black stone, very low)
- Steam vents creating white drifting columns
- The Forge as the dominant building
- Ash-haze sky, orange-grey, no clean air

**Palette:** Basalt `#201818`, lava `#E05020` / `#FF7010`, ash sky `#706060`, steam white `#D0C8C0`, forge building `#382828`, sulphur `#C0A020`

### Image 10: `the_anchorage.png`

**Scene:** A tiny fishing village on a windswept coastal promontory. Four or five weathered buildings facing into the wind. Fishing boats dragged above the tide line. A telescope on a tripod outside one building (the researchers). Salt-bleached driftwood fences. Wind-bent trees lean permanently away from the coast. The sea is enormous and close in every direction except one narrow land connection. Overcast dusk.

**Mood:** Small, exposed, at the absolute edge of the known world. Ancient and quiet.

**Key elements:**
- Tiny cluster of buildings on a promontory
- Fishing boats above the tide line (upturned or dragged)
- A telescope on a tripod — distinctive detail
- Wind-bent trees all leaning the same direction
- Dark sea surrounding on three sides, very present

**Palette:** Sea dark blue-grey `#3A5060`, driftwood `#8A8068`, building stone `#706860`, overcast sky `#6A7888`, bent tree dark `#2A3820`, telescope brass `#A08020`

### Image 11: `the_holdfast.png`

**Scene:** Ash grey scorched earth as far as the eye can see. The Holdfast is a fortified ruin — walls half-crumbled but patched with salvaged stone and timber barricades. Inside the walls: campfires casting warm orange light on exhausted soldier silhouettes. In the far distance, on the horizon, the sky is WRONG — a darkness beyond darkness where the Fading consumes everything, stars disappearing into it. It is the absence of light, not darkness — like a hole in the sky. The campfires inside the Holdfast walls are the only warmth in the image.

**Mood:** Last stand, desperate defiance. The fires burn because people refuse to stop.

**Key elements:**
- Ash grey and scorched earth everywhere
- The Holdfast walls — crumbling but reinforced with salvage
- Campfires inside with soldier silhouettes
- THE VOID on the horizon — absolute dark, edges of stars disappearing into it
- Contrast: warm fire glow vs absolute cold darkness beyond

**Palette:** Ash earth `#605850`, void `#000000` (true black), campfire `#C06020`, crumbling stone `#787068`, soot `#201818`, soldier silhouette `#1A1410`, dim sky `#2A2820` with void eating the right side
